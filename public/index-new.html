Rustracer

Rustracer is a memory safe, multi-threaded raytracer written from scratch in Rust.

Features:

  • Camera movement
  • Dynamic focal length
  • Metallic, glass, and lambertian materials
  • Thread tailorability
  • Scene saving and loading
